Title: CHTF Opening Ceremony_CHINA HI-TECH FAIRCHINA HI-TECH FAIR
Date: None
URL: http://www.chtf.com/english/Conferences/ChinaHTF/

The opening ceremony of the 26th China High-Tech Fair will be held at the Shenzhen World Exhibition and Convention Center on November 14, 2024. It is scheduled to last 20 to 30 minutes. There will be an introduction of the officials and guests attending the event, the speech session, and related topics, etc. The pavilion tour session will start after the opening ceremony.
