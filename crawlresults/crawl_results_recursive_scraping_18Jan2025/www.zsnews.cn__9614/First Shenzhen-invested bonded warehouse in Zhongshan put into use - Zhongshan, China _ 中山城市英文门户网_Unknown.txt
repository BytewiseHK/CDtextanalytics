Title: First Shenzhen-invested bonded warehouse in Zhongshan put into use - Zhongshan, China | 中山城市英文门户网
Date: None
URL: https://en.zsnews.cn/Business/ForeignTrade/content/380/42520.html

The Shunye Supply Chain Public Bonded Warehouse was unveiled and put into use on December 26. Two trucks, loaded with high-end LCD screens, entered the 9,000-square-meter bonded warehouse for customs inspection, marking the official operation of the first bonded warehouse in Zhongshan invested and established by a Shenzhen enterprise.
"The Shunye bonded warehouse will provide upstream and downstream enterprises with one-stop service covering customs declaration, warehousing, distribution and finance, with an estimated annual import and export value exceeding 2 billion yuan. It is expected to save over 6 million yuan for clients every year", said Lyu Liangliang, general manager of Zhongshan Shunye Supply Chain Management Co.
At the unveiling ceremony, Zhongshan Customs introduced a series of supporting measures for high-end display manufacturing, such as supporting investment attraction in high-end display industrial parks, supporting the integrated development of high-end display industry chain between Shenzhen and Zhongshan and etc, so as to form a coordinated development pattern of "Headquarters in Shenzhen + Manufacturing in Zhongshan".
