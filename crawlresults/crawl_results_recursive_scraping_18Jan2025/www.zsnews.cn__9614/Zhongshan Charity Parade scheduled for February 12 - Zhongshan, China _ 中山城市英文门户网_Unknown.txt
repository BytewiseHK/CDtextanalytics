Title: Zhongshan Charity Parade scheduled for February 12 - Zhongshan, China | 中山城市英文门户网
Date: None
URL: https://en.zsnews.cn/CULTUREEDUCATION/LatestEvents/content/382/42576.html

The 38th Zhongshan Charity Parade will be held at Xingzhong Avenue on the morning of February 12, also the Lantern Festival on the fifteen day of the first lunar month.
It has opened for online registrations until January 22.
Participants must be donors who have made donations to the Red Cross Society of China Zhongshan Branch or related activities at the town or sub-district level.
A maximum of 280 parade teams (not including performance teams) will be allowed to take part in the event.
