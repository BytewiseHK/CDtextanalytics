In December 2024, the One Voice Children's Choir from the United States embarked on their second concert tour in China, with Shenzhen as the first stop. During their three-day visit, what brought tears to the children's eyes? And as the New Year approached, what wishes did they make for the year ahead?
2024年12月，美國的One Voice兒童合唱團開啟了他們第二次中國巡演，而深圳是此次巡演的首站。在為期三天的深圳之行中，是什麼讓孩子們熱淚盈眶?在新年即將到來之際，他們又為未來許下了怎樣的願望?
