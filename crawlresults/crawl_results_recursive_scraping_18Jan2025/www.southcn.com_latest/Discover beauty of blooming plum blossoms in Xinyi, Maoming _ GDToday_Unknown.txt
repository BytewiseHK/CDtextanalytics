Title: Discover beauty of blooming plum blossoms in Xinyi, Maoming | GDToday
Date: None
URL: https://www.newsgd.com/node_99363c4f3b/90c05dec08.shtml

Recently, Xinyi in Maoming City has become breathtaking as plum blossoms bloom across multiple locations, including Yingdipo Village, Pudong Village, and Shangmo Village. The vibrant plum blossoms paint a picturesque landscape and highlight the region's rich agricultural charm. This seasonal phenomenon attracts nature enthusiasts and photographers alike, offering a serene escape into nature's splendor.
