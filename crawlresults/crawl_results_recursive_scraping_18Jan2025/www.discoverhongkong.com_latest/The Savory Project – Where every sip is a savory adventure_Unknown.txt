Title: The Savory Project – Where every sip is a savory adventure
Date: None
URL: https://thesavoryproject.com/

We are a unique and ever-evolving bar concept located in the heart of Hong Kong’s Soho district. Founded by the award-winning team behind COA, The Savory Project is a celebration of complex and nuanced flavors.
Our affable bartenders meticulously craft cocktails that feature unorthodox ingredients and emphasize savory and umami notes. We also offer a range of specially crafted non-alcoholic drinks, creating an equitable space that is enjoyable for everyone.
Come and join us at The Savory Project and experience the future of cocktail culture.
