Title: Hong Kong’s Nicholas Tse to put on 2 Kai Tak concerts soon after Coldplay | South China Morning Post
Date: Published:9:07pm, 15 Jan 2025
URL: https://www.scmp.com/news/hong-kong/hong-kong-economy/article/3294916/hong-kongs-nicholas-tse-put-2-kai-tak-concerts-soon-after-coldplay#comments

Concerts will be held at 50,000-seat Kai Tak Stadium on April 25 and 26, with ticket prices starting at HK$680
Tse announced his “Evolution Nic Live” shows on social media on Wednesday in a video of himself playing guitar at the stadium. The concerts will take place at Kai Tak Stadium on April 25 and 26 at 7.30pm, with ticket prices starting at HK$680.
The park, the city’s first multipurpose sports, entertainment and leisure precinct, will officially open in March, with the Hong Kong Sevens rugby tournament the first event fully open to the public.
