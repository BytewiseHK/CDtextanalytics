Title: Hong Kong police officer hurts hand after gun fired accidentally in station | South China Morning Post
Date: Published:7:53pm, 11 Jan 2025
URL: https://www.scmp.com/news/hong-kong/society/article/3294374/hong-kong-police-officer-hurts-hand-after-gun-fired-accidentally-station

Officer was unloading firearm in Happy Valley Police Station when it accidentally discharged, source says
A Hong Kong police officer injured her hand and was sent to hospital on Saturday after her gun was accidentally discharged in a station, the Post learned.
The officer was unloading her service pistol at Happy Valley Police Station when it accidentally fired one round at about 3pm, a source said.
She injured her fingers and was sent to Ruttonjee Hospital in Wan Chai for treatment, the insider said, adding that no one else was hurt.
