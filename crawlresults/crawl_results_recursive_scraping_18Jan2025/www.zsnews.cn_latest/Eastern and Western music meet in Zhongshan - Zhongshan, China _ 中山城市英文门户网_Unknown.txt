Title: Eastern and Western music meet in Zhongshan - Zhongshan, China | 中山城市英文门户网
Date: None
URL: https://en.zsnews.cn/CULTUREEDUCATION/LatestEvents/content/382/42552.html

Members ofthe New Philharmonia Orchestra of Munich, including violinists Irene Ballesteros and Claudio Ordoñez and Chinese German young violist Wang Xinyuan, together with the Lingnan Chamber Traditional Orchestra of Zhongshan, visited Zhongshan Shiqi Central Primary School for music exchanges on January 6.
During the exchange performance, the New Philharmonia Orchestra of Munich performed "I Love You, China", a Chinese folk song, with Western music style, while the Lingnan Chamber Traditional Orchestra of Zhongshan performed "The Radesky March" with Chinese folk music style. The cross-cultural music show let the audience experience the charm of "music without borders".
Previously on the evening of January 3, the debut performance of the New Philharmonia Orchestra of Munich China Tour 2025 was staged at Zhongshan Culture & Art Center. The artists conveyed their best wishes for the New Year to the audience in Zhongshan through beautiful melodies.
