Shagangxu Market
Date: January 19 to 28
Venue: Eastern Shagangxu Market
With a history of over 20 years, the Shagangxu flower fair has become a must-visit place for many Zhongshan residents during the Spring Festival.
Tanzhou Town
Date: January 18 to 28
Venue 1: Tanzhou Civil Square
Venue 2: Car Park 1 of 14 Creative Cultural Loft
Nantou Town
Date: January 11 to 28
Venue: Nantou Can Art Garden
It boasts areas for New Year plants, Spring Festival couplets and groceries, catering, cultural and creative products, and performances.
Huangpu Town
Date: January 14 to 28 (New Year plants and Spring Festival couplets)
January 24 to 28 (food)
Venue: Huangpu International Convention and Exhibition Center
Dongfeng Town
Date: January 19 to 28 (9:00-22:00, and 9:00-17:00 on January 28)
Venue: Yufeng Road, Bogong Community
It is a combination of fine food, shopping, festive performances and creative activities.
Guzhen Town
Date: January 17 to 28
Venue: Guzhen Convention and Exhibition Center
Sanjiao Town
Date: January 15 to 28
Venue: Near Hexie Road
Booths will be set up to sell New Year plants, bonsai, Spring Festival groceries, snacks, etc.
