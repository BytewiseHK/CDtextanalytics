Great Shots of the Week is a weekly photo solicitation event held by Zhongshan Daily to collect photos from the public every week. Winners will be given prizes and electronic certificates. All residents including expats are welcome to participate and record beautiful moments in Zhongshan every week. Winners of the week (December 16 to December 22) are as follows.
Taekwondo Show: December 20, the Xiaze Primary School Sports Meeting opens in Shaxi Town. The picture shows students staging a wonderful taekwondo show. (By Zhang Dapeng)
Catching a Fat Duck: December 17, a farm owner is catching ducks in Tanzhou Town. (By Gao Xingtong)
Fairytale World: December 17, Changjiang Reservoirwith bald cypress treeslooks like a fairyland world under the morning sun. (By Mi Li)
Soaring Dragon: December 17, at the Xiaolan Chrysanthemum Fair, the 188-meter "Vatti Lucky Dragon" floral landscape is eye-catching. (By Su Mushi)
Ping Pong Fans: December 18, table tennis lovers are playing under the setting sun at Dongmingqiao Aquatic Plant Park. (By Wu Guanhong)
Perching: December 16, by the Xijiang River in Banfu Town, egrets return to their nests at dusk. (By Liu Shijue)
