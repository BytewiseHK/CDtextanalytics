Twenty years ago, fishing was the main livelihood of Jiangmen Dongfeng Village. Since the introduction of South American white shrimp, the village's collective income in 2023 has doubled compared to 2017. How did Dongfeng Village use "a single shrimp" to boost the village's collective economy? Today, follow Paul Farrelly as he explores.
