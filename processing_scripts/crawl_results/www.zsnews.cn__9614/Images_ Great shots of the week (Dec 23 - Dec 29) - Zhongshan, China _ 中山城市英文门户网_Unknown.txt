Great Shots of the Week is a weekly photo solicitation event held by Zhongshan Daily to collect photos from the public every week. Winners will be given prizes and electronic certificates. All residents including expats are welcome to participate and record beautiful moments in Zhongshan every week. Winners of the week (December 23 to December 29) are as follows.
Fierce Competition:Evening of December 26, 2024, the 11th Zhongshan Men's Basketball League kicks off at the Zhongshan Fitness Square. The photo shows thefierce matchbetween Team Guzhen and Team Nanlang. (By Chen Xiaoping)
ThrillingRoller Coaster: December 28, 2024,the Zhongshan OCT Harbour Joy Land parkopens, attracting a large number of residents and tourists. (By Wu Guanhong)
Birds Fly Back: Afternoon of December 28, 2024, many water birds are taking a rest by the Modaomen Waterway, enjoying the tranquil beauty of the winter season. (By Xi Jiang)
Pickle Drying: December 29, 2024, Guanjiawei villagers in Huangpu Town are joyfully drying their pickles in the warm sun. (By Zhuo Jianghua)
Trying on Earrings: December 28, 2024, at the Holiday Plaza fair, a lady is trying on her favorite earrings. (By Yang Yaobin)
Dilapidated: December 28, 2024, a group of volunteers collaborate to demolish a dilapidated shack in Fusha Town. (By Luo Binlun)
