Covering 67,610 square meters, Qiaocheng North Park is located to the south of Antuo Hill Park. With lush trees and grass, Qiaocheng North Park integrates hilly terrain, recreational areas and fitness facilities within the natural ecological environment.

Metro: Line 2 to Qiaocheng North Station (侨城北站), Exit C
