Title: 'Folk Song Festival' held in the city | EYESHENZHEN
Date: None
URL: https://www.eyeshenzhen.com/content/2025-01/17/content_31441644.htm

On the evening of Jan. 16, “My City, My Song-Folk Song Festival (Season 2)” was held at NUBOND AIR, Houhai Harbour, Nanshan. Sixteen folk musicians from Xi'an, Shenzhen, Shantou and other cities presented the ballads at the folk music concert. The finale of the “Folk Song Festival” will be presented Jan. 18 at Futian Sports Park and free tickets will be offered.
