Title: Tang Wei makes shining comeback at Cannes
Date: None

CHINESE actress Tang Wei made a stunning comeback in South Korean director’s twisted love story “Decision to Leave,” which premiered at this year’s Cannes and snatched the best director award.Starring Tang and South Korean actor Park Hae-il, “Decision to Leave” tells the story of a detective who, investigating a man’s fatal fall from a mountain, comes under the spell of the victim’s wife, whom he suspects of having caused her husband’s death.The movie was warmly received by Cannes audiences. CHINESE actress Tang Wei made a stunning comeback in South Korean director’s twisted love story “Decision to Leave,” which premiered at this year’s Cannes and snatched the best director award.
Starring Tang and South Korean actor Park Hae-il, “Decision to Leave” tells the story of a detective who, investigating a man’s fatal fall from a mountain, comes under the spell of the victim’s wife, whom he suspects of having caused her husband’s death.
The movie was warmly received by Cannes audiences. The BBC called it a “cracking romantic thriller” and Screen said that “Park crafts a deeply satisfying and potentially commercial thriller.”
(SD-Agencies)
