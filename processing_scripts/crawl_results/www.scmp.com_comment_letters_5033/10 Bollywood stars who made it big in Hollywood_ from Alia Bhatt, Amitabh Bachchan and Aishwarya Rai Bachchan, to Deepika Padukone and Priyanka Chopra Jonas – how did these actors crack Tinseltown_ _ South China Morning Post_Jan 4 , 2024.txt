While Indian stars have been making a mark in the West for decades, and have been seamlessly transitioning between industries, they also have been breaking stereotypes and expanding the global influence of Indian cinema. Not limited to film, Indian voices are increasingly prominent in TV shows, streaming projects and various creative endeavours, leaving an indelible mark in the global entertainment space with their diverse roles.
Read on to get to know 10 Indian stars who have seamlessly transitioned to the West.
Deepika Padukone made her stunning debut in the Hindi film industry in 2008 withOm Shanti Om, starring opposite Indian superstar Shah Rukh Khan. With an impressive 17-year career, she is one of the most popular leading stars in India.
In just 11 years, Alia Bhatt has become one of the top female stars in India, captivating audiences with her versatility and critically acclaimed performances. She has gained recognition for her emotionally charged roles in films likeHighway(2014),Udta Punjab(2016),Dear Zindagi(2016),Gully Boy(2019),Darlings(2022) andGangubai Kathiawadi(2022).
