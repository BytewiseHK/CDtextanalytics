Title: Third session of  14th Guangdong Provincial People's Congress concludes in Guangzhou | GDToday
Date: None
URL: https://www.newsgd.com/node_5c070fdd03/86b9a76413.shtml

The third session of the 14th Guangdong Provincial People's Congress concluded in Guangzhou on the morning of January 18.
The Guangdong Government Work Report, Work Report of the Standing Committee of Guangdong Provincial People's Congress, Work Report of the Guangdong Provincial People's Procuratorate, Work Report of the Higher People's Court of Guangdong Province and Draft Decision on the Implementation of the Plan for Guangdong's Economic and Social Development in 2024 and on the 2025 Plan, as well as Draft Decision on the Budgetary Performance of Guangdong in 2024 and on the Budget in 2025 were approved after being voted by the deputies.
Furthermore, the establishment of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Standing Committee of Guangdong Provincial People's Congress was announced at the meeting.
